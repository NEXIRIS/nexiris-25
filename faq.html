General Information

What is Nexiris Tech Fest?
Nexiris Tech Fest is an annual technology festival that brings together students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to explore the latest innovations, participate in competitions, attend workshops, and network with industry leaders. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of innovators and creators.
When and where will Nexiris be held?
Nexiris Tech Fest 2025 will take place from October 27th to October 29th at the Grand Innovation Center, Tech Park Avenue. The venue is centrally located with excellent public transportation access and ample parking facilities.
Who can attend Nexiris Tech Fest?
Nexiris is open to everyone interested in technology! This includes students from high schools and universities, working professionals, educators, researchers, and technology enthusiasts. Some workshops and competitions may have specific eligibility requirements which will be clearly mentioned in their descriptions.

Registration & Tickets

How do I register for Nexiris?
Registration can be completed through our official website. Simply go to the Registration section, choose your attendee type (student, professional, or group), select the events you wish to participate in, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your digital pass.
Are there different ticket types?
Yes, we offer several ticket options:
  • • Student Pass: Access to all main events and exhibitions
  • • Professional Pass: Includes workshops and networking sessions
  • • VIP Pass: Priority access, exclusive sessions, and lounge access
  • • Group Pass: Discounted rates for groups of 5 or more
Early bird discounts are available until September 30th.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend?
Refunds are available up to 30 days before the event starts. After this date, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another person by updating the registration details through our website. Please contact our support team for assistance with transfers.

Events & Activities

What kind of events can I expect?
Nexiris offers a diverse range of activities including:
  • • Technical workshops (AI, Blockchain, IoT, Cybersecurity)
  • • Innovation challenges and hackathons
  • • Keynote speeches from industry pioneers
  • • Panel discussions on emerging technologies
  • • Tech exhibitions showcasing cutting-edge products
  • • Networking sessions with tech companies
  • • Career fair with recruitment opportunities
  • • Startup pitch competitions
Do I need prior experience to participate in workshops?
Our workshops are designed for various skill levels. Each workshop description includes information about the required skill level:
  • • Beginner: No prior experience needed
  • • Intermediate: Basic understanding of the topic
  • • Advanced: Significant experience required
We recommend reviewing workshop prerequisites before registering to ensure you select sessions appropriate for your skill level.

Participation & Teams

Can I participate in multiple events?
Absolutely! You can participate in multiple events as long as their schedules don't conflict. When registering, you'll be able to see the event timetable and select the sessions you wish to attend. Some competitions may have participation limits to ensure fair access for all attendees.
How do I form a team for competitions?
For team-based competitions, you can:
  • • Register with a pre-formed team during event registration
  • • Find teammates at our "Team Formation" session on Day 1
  • • Use our online platform to connect with other participants
Each competition specifies team size requirements. Most hackathons allow teams of 3-5 members.
What should I bring to the event?
We recommend bringing:
  • • Your printed ticket or digital pass
  • • Government-issued ID for verification
  • • Laptop and charger (for workshops and competitions)
  • • Notebook and pen
  • • Reusable water bottle
  • • Portable power bank
For hackathon participants, any specialized hardware or equipment you might need for your project.
